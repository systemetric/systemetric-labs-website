Systemetric is a robotics team comprising students aged 16–18 from Hills Road Sixth Form College, Cambridge, England. We have been competing in robotics competitions since 2002, starting with the FIRST Robotics Competition in the USA; we now participate in the annual Student Robotics competition based at Southampton University.
